Meet The Articles

The Articles are (were) a Detroit-area ska/jazz fusion band who mix covers of jazz standards, as well as ska classics, with their originals. The group was founded by brothers Derek (trumpet) and Paul Phelps (guitar), with the remainder of the original lineup consisting of alto saxophonist Mike Rehfus, tenor saxophonist Sean Stillwell, bassist Jim Hohner, and drummer Dan Margulis. Their debut album, Flip F'Real, was released in 1997 by Moon Ska

After several lineup changes, the updated group recorded a new album of all original material in March of 2001. Unfortunately, the group disbanded before the album saw a proper release.

After 20 long years, The Articles are excited to announce that this album will soon be available on all streaming services! Titled "Action Claw vs. Gorilla Grip", the recordings show the evolution of the group's music, and offer a glimpse of what might have been...
